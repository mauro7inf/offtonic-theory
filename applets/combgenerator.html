Offtonic Comb Generator

This is the Offtonic Comb Generator. Enter two frequencies, a number of teeth, and a volume, and it will generate that number of evenly spaced pitches as pure sine waves. For example, if you enter a low frequency of 220 Hz, a high frequency of 880 Hz, and 3 teeth, there will be a tone generated at 220 Hz, one at 440 Hz, and another at 880 Hz. The sound will continue until you hit Stop, even if you switch to another tab.

Why would I ever want to do this?

Meh, not my problem. But I'll tell you what I used it for: I generated a bunch of frequencies at a very low volume to use as a kind of ruler in a spectrograph. I recorded the comb together with a track I wanted to analyze, and in Audacity's spectrograph, I was able to figure out the pitch in the track by comparing it with the lines made by the comb. I thought it was clever. You can use the comb for whatever you want, though.
